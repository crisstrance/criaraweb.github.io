12 Years Project is composed of twelve panels, each representing a month worked on over the course of twelve years. This project is composed of drawings, sketches, and notes, completed daily over the span of one month each year, during 12 years. It began in January 2013, during an erasmus in Maastricht, continued in February 2014, March 2015, and so on, until the project was completed on December 31, 2024. Conceived as an exercise in self-discovery and creative discipline, aiming to capture the artist's technical, aesthetic, and personal evolution, reflecting on the passage of time and constant change.

THE ONLY RULE: Each drawing had to be completed within 24 hours of the given day.

A rule that underscores the commitment to capturing the immediacy of the moment. This rule also led to the inclusion of gaps in the panels for days when no drawing was made, serving as a visual statement about the impossibility of absolute control over everything.
